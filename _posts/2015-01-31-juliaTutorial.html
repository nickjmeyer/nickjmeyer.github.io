Arithmetic 

x = 8
y = 7
print(x)
print(y)
print(x+y)

8715

Wait, what happened here? The output of x,y,x+y is mashed together. How to fix this? 

x = 8
y = 7
print(x,"\n")
print(y,"\n")
print(x+y,"\n")

8
7
15

While it may be someone cumbersome to type the newline character "\n" everytime, this setup helps keep your output customizable.
